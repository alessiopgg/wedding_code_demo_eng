Travel & Accommodation

Helpful notes for guests travelling from out of town

We recommend booking accommodation early, especially if you plan to stay in central Florence.

Recommended Hotels

  • Hotel Palazzo Bianca — 10 min from venue
  • Relais Santa Maria — boutique option in the center
  • Villa Serena Suites — quiet countryside stay

Transport

  • Florence Airport: approx. 30 minutes by car
  • Santa Maria Novella Station: approx. 20 minutes by taxi
  • Evening shuttle available after reception

Logistics

  • Check-in the day before is highly recommended
  • Taxis should be booked in advance for late return
  • Weather in June is usually warm — bring a light layer for the evening
